1. Plain-English Product Definition
Inventory Funding Manager - Distribution helps a distributor decide how much inventory can be responsibly funded now, which purchase candidates deserve that funding first, which should be reduced, split, delayed, held, or declined, and which decisions require reviewer or owner approval.
Locked Hard Rules
IFM is source-agnostic. QuickBooks and Keystroke are example/test sources, not required systems.
IFM maps source data into standard IFM data domains before calculations run.
IFM does not create, send, modify, cancel, pay, borrow, or factor automatically in V1.5.
Funding approval is separate from purchase approval and separate from official PO action.
Buyer action in Keystroke or another purchasing system happens outside IFM in V1.5.
LOC and factoring are supplemental funding, not operating cash.
Vendor deals, discounts, free freight, and extended terms are not automatically good.
RSE identifies replenishment need and inventory classification; IFM allocates funding priority.
Blank or missing data must not be treated as zero unless confirmed none exist.
Any file intended for Keystroke upload or re-import must be tab-delimited and end in .txt.
CSV/XLSX may be used for review, mapping, QA, internal analysis, or customer review, but not as Keystroke import-ready files.
Customer-facing output must protect sensitive internal, financing, AR, AP, and reviewer detail unless approved for display.
2. Core Business Question
Based on cash, obligations, AP, AR, open POs, financing options, vendor terms, inventory position, service risk, and purchase evidence, which inventory purchases can we responsibly fund now, reduce, delay, split, or hold?

10. Fundable vs. Recommended
A purchase can be technically fundable but not recommended. IFM must show both funding status and recommendation status.

11. V1.5 Developer Instructions
Build standard IFM domains first; do not build QuickBooks-only or Keystroke-only logic.
Create source_systems, source_documents, upload_batches, field_mappings, and standard_ifm_domains structures.
Build calculations from standardized IFM tables, not raw source-system files.
Keep thresholds configurable: reserve, owner approval, AR confidence factors, vendor concentration, holdback, material purchase amount.
Build the Purchase Funding Decision Board as the main working screen.
Use Document 7 sample test data and Document 8 acceptance checklist before pilot.
